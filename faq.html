What Is Fam Bam?

Fam Bam is a personalized platform for which you and your family can stay connected on. Not only can you send and receive messages, but you can also track the progress of your interactions.

Help Page

How Do I Send a Message?

Click on the button on the home page with under the contact you wish to message. You can also press the [plus sign] icon on top to send any of your contact a message.

What Are Interaction Statistics?

The interaction statistics page shows a visual of the amount of times you and your family member has interacted throughout the year. There are also suggestions given based on the frequency of interaction to keep you motivated on communicating.

How Do I See A Family Member's Profile?

Click on the button in their contact card.

What Do The Different Borders Around Each Contact On The Home Page Mean?

Each color represents the availability status of each of your contacts.

A red border means the contact is:

Unavailable



A green border means the contact is:

Available
